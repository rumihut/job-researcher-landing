- Goal: Build personal brand as AI/automation leader → attract better opportunities → transition out of Oracle with a promotion
- Key concern: Don't want Oracle colleagues to think side projects mean less focus on the job
- Resolution: Reframe — "I do AI at Oracle AND I ship AI products to stay sharp" = power move, not risk

## Positioning
**Core identity:** AI & Automation professional who doesn't just talk about AI — he builds with it

**NOT:** "I'm building a side business to escape"
**YES:** "I'm so passionate about AI that I build products on nights and weekends to learn what's actually possible"

This makes Aaron:
- More valuable to Oracle (they should want to keep him)
- More attractive to external companies (proven builder, not just talker)
- Authentic (you can't fake actually shipping products)

## Content Framework (3x/week)

### Schedule
- **Monday** — "What I Learned" (specific, vulnerable, lesson-driven)
- **Wednesday** — "Contrarian Take" (challenge conventional wisdom with evidence)
- **Friday** — "Behind the Scenes" (build story with data, timelines, failures)

### 7 Elements of High-Performing Posts (score each post 0-7)
1. Specific opening data point
2. Personal narrative arc (beginning, conflict, resolution)
3. Vulnerable admission (messy reality, not sanitized)
4. Contrarian insight (challenge conventional wisdom)
5. Specific evidence (dollar amounts, dates, numbers)
6. Practical application (what can people do with this?)
7. Authentic voice (write like you talk)

Target: 5+ per post. Below 3 = don't post it.

### What Doesn't Matter (stop optimizing these)
- ❌ Posting time (4% variance — post whenever)
- ❌ Hashtags (0-1 is best, 5+ hurts reach by 84%)
- ❌ Post frequency (quality > quantity, 3x/week is plenty)
- ❌ Images/carousels (text-only outperforms for dwell time)
- ❌ Asking for engagement (71% worse reach)
- ❌ Short posts (1,200+ words outperforms by 18x)

### What DOES Matter
- ✅ **Dwell time** — the #1 algorithm signal. Make people stop and read.
- ✅ **Specific details** — times, amounts, dates, real conversations
- ✅ **Vulnerability** — share the messy reality, not the highlight reel
- ✅ **Contrarian takes** — 3.2x more reach when backed with evidence
- ✅ **First-hour engagement** — reply to every comment within 60 minutes

## Engagement Strategy (15 min/day)
1. **Find 10-15 target accounts** — AI leaders, automation builders, startup founders, hiring managers at target companies
2. **Leave substantive comments** — add data, share experience, ask questions. Never "Great post!"
3. **Comment BEFORE you post** — this warms up the algorithm for your own content
4. **Reply to every comment** on your posts within 60 minutes
5. **Connection requests** — after someone engages, connect with a note referencing their comment
